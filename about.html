About Me

Hi! I'm Alex, a visual storyteller and creative based in Colorado. For the past 8 years, I've been exploring the world through my lens, capturing moments that speak to the human experience - from quiet contemplative scenes to dynamic urban landscapes.

My journey started with curiosity and a simple camera. What began as personal documentation has evolved into a passion for visual narrative. I'm drawn to authentic moments, experimental techniques, and collaborative projects that push creative boundaries.

I've collaborated with fellow artists, documented cultural events, and created experimental pieces that explore the intersection of technology and art. Each project is an opportunity to learn, grow, and connect with others who share a passion for visual storytelling.

My approach is intuitive: observe, connect, and create. I believe the most powerful visuals emerge from genuine moments and authentic collaboration with others.
