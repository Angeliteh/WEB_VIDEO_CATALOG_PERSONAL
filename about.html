About Me

Hi! I'm Alex, a passionate videographer and photographer based in Colorado. For the past 8 years, I've been capturing stories through my lens - from intimate wedding moments to breathtaking landscapes, corporate events to personal adventures.

My journey started with a simple camera and a love for adventure. Today, I specialize in creating cinematic content that tells authentic stories. Whether it's documenting your special day, creating promotional content for your business, or collaborating on creative projects, I bring passion and professionalism to every shoot.

I've had the privilege of working with amazing clients across various industries - from local businesses to destination weddings, music videos to documentary projects. Each project teaches me something new and fuels my creativity.

My approach is simple: listen, understand, and create. I believe the best content comes from genuine connections and understanding what makes each story unique.
